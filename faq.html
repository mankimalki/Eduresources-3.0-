Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers about our free resources and affordable tutoring for UK students

Are all resources really free?

Yes! Every resource on EduResources, including past papers, mark schemes, and study guides for GCSE, A-Level, and IGCSE, is completely free to access and download. We believe quality education should be accessible to everyone.

Which exam boards do you cover?

We support all major UK boards: AQA, Edexcel, OCR, WJEC, and Cambridge. Resources are available for GCSE, A-Level, and IGCSE subjects.

How often do you add new materials?

We add new resources weekly, especially around exam seasons. This includes the latest past papers, updated mark schemes, and new study guides.

Do I need an account to download?

No account is needed for most resources. However, creating a free account lets you save favorites, track your progress, and get personalized study recommendations.

Are mark schemes included?

Yes, we provide mark schemes with past papers whenever available. Some older papers might not have mark schemes, but we strive to offer them for the most recent exams.

Can I contribute resources?

Yes! We welcome contributions from teachers and students. Visit our Contribute page to share your high-quality materials and help fellow learners.

How do you keep this free?

We're funded by donations from users like you and educational grants. Our mission is to keep essential education resources accessible to all students.

Do you offer tutoring services?

Yes, we offer premium tutoring services at very affordable rates. Our expert tutors provide personalized support for GCSE, A-Level, and IGCSE subjects across all major UK exam boards. Contact us to learn more and find a tutor that fits your needs.
