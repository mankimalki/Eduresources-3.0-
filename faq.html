Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our free educational resources

Are all resources really free?

Yes! Every resource on EduResources is completely free to access and download. We believe education should be available to everyone.

Which exam boards do you cover?

We support all major UK boards: AQA, Edexcel, OCR, WJEC, and Cambridge. Resources are available for GCSE, A-Level, and IGCSE.

How often do you add new materials?

We add new resources weekly, especially around exam seasons. This includes past papers, mark schemes, and study guides.

Do I need an account to download?

No account is needed for most resources. However, creating a free account lets you save favorites and track progress.

Are mark schemes included?

Yes, we provide mark schemes with past papers whenever possible. Some older papers may not have mark schemes available.

Can I contribute resources?

Yes! We welcome contributions from teachers and students. Visit our Contribute page to share your materials.

How do you keep this free?

We're funded by donations and educational grants. Our mission is to keep education accessible to all students.

Still have questions? We're here to help!
