Frequently Asked Questions

Check our FAQ section below for quick answers to common questions.

How can I access your free resources?
All our resources are available for free download on our website. Simply navigate to the "Resources" or "Past Papers" section, select what you need, and download directly to your device.
Do I need to create an account to download materials?
No, we don't require any accounts or registrations. All resources are freely accessible without any login requirements.
How can I support EduResources?
We appreciate any support! You can donate through our WhatsApp number, share our resources with others, or volunteer to help us expand our collection. Every contribution helps us maintain and grow our free services.
Can I request specific resources?
Absolutely! We welcome resource requests. Please use our contact form or WhatsApp to let us know what materials you need, and we'll do our best to add them to our collection.
